Advantages

  • Effectively removes stubborn dirt, does not leave marks and streaks.
  • Significantly facilitates subsequent cleaning.
  • Does not contain stubborn components such as silicones, cationic surfactants, acrylic film formers.

Application

Before use, thoroughly vacuum the surface, check the compatibility of the product with the processed materials in an inconspicuous area. The addition of specialized defoamers permitted for this use is allowed. Apply to the surface with a spray gun. Soak for 1-2 minutes. Wipe stubborn dirt with a specialized sponge, brush or napkin, without applying significant force. Collect the rest of the solution from the surface with a vacuum cleaner, an extractor or a dry wiping material.

Dilution:Trigger: the product is ready to use. Manual method with trigger / spray gun: 1: 5 – 1:20 Vacuum cleaner: 1: 6 – 1: 9

Additional information

Storage conditions: store in a tightly closed original container at a temperature of + 5˚C to + 25˚C. After defrosting and thorough mixing, it completely restores its properties.Expiration date: 3 years.

Composition: deionized water (30% or more), nonionic surfactants (more than 5% but less than 15%), complexing agents (less than 5%), water-soluble solvents (less than 5%)., Preservative (less than 5%)., Flavoring additives (less than 5%).
